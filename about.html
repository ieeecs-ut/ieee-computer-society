About

The IEEE Computer Society is the world's leading computing membership organization and the trusted information and career-development source for a global workforce of technology leaders. Members have endless opportunities to network with the world's foremost technology professionals. At the University of Texas, we collaborate and share information on the most up-to-date technologies and innovations in the computer world. We are dedicated to advancing the theory and application of computer and information-processing technology.

As a branch of the IEEE organization, IEEE CS constantly has presentations by corporations and companies on their latest technologies, lectures to assist in the learning of all UT students, and socials that allow for every CS member to know and cooperate with one another in various projects and competitions.

Interested in joining? Just sign up for our email list, here. There are no member fees or forms, just attend our events, eat our food, and have a blast!
