Girl Day at UT

IEEE Computer Society is committed to inspiring future engineers and helping them develop a passion for STEM. This year, our organization hosted a coding event at Girl Day to show how art and technology can be combined in creative ways. Find more information about the event here.

Explore UT

Explore UT is an exciting opportunity for K-12 students to discover our university's passion for knowledge through live demonstrations, interactive events, and more! Our members support this event by volunteering and hosting events.

Supporting Our Community

IEEE Computer Society is dedicated to supporting our Longhorn community especially during difficult times like this pandemic. Our goal is to provide fellow students with useful academic, social, and financial resources to help them succeed!

UT Survival Guide

Ever wonder what ECE classes are like or how different professors teach them? Want to know some useful tips for scoring your first internship? Are you interested in graduate school and are curious about what steps you should take? If you answered yes to any of these questions, check out our comprehensive survival guide that our officers made!

UT Interview Guide

Check out our interview guide to learn about how you can ace your interviews and land your dream job! Our officers created a comprehensive set of tips to help network with recruiters at career fairs and prepare you for behavioral and technical interviews for internships and full-time positions.
